Seasons

New Work by Joan Branca at the Union League Club

The Union League Club
38 East 37th Street
New York, NY 10016

Business dress requested

Join us for a reception heralding the opening of "Seasons", featuring the work of Joan Branca. Along Long Island's north shore, Ms. Branca fixes in paint her experience of a sense of place. Weaving a tapestry of color and light, Ms. Branca invites the viewer into the canvas to observe the rhythms and atmosphere of coastal scenes. The artist works both “en plein air” and in her Long Island studio, creating vibrant oil paintings that are a captivating expression of the beauty and mystery of nature in all seasons.
